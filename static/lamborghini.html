Lamborghini Revuelto

The Lamborghini Revuelto marks a bold new era for the iconic brand as its first production hybrid supercar. Powered by a naturally aspirated V12 engine paired with three electric motors, it produces over 1,000 horsepower, delivering blistering acceleration and sharp responsiveness. The Revuelto combines Lamborghini’s signature angular, aggressive design with state-of-the-art aerodynamics and advanced chassis dynamics. Inside, it features a digital cockpit and luxurious finishes that reflect the brand’s racing heritage and commitment to innovation. With a 0–60 mph time under 2.5 seconds, the Revuelto redefines what a modern Lamborghini can be—ferocious, futuristic, and undeniably elite.

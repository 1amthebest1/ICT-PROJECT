Bugatti Bolide

The Bugatti Bolide is a radical, track-focused hypercar that pushes the limits of performance and engineering. Unveiled as a limited-production masterpiece, the Bolide is powered by Bugatti’s legendary 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine, producing an astonishing 1,577 horsepower. With a curb weight of just 1,450 kg (3,197 lbs), it achieves an unprecedented power-to-weight ratio, enabling blistering acceleration and top-tier handling. The Bolide’s aerodynamics are extreme, featuring massive wings, active surfaces, and a futuristic, minimalist cockpit built solely for high-speed driving. Limited to just 40 units, this $4.3 million hypercar is one of the most exclusive and extreme Bugattis ever made.

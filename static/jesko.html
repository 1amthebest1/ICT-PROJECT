Koenigsegg Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko is a Swedish-engineered hypercar that pushes the boundaries of automotive performance. Powered by a 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, it produces 1,280 horsepower on standard fuel and up to 1,600 horsepower when running on E85 biofuel . The Jesko is available in two variants: the Attack, optimized for maximum downforce and track performance, and the Absolut, designed for top-speed runs with minimal aerodynamic drag. Notably, the Jesko Absolut set a world record by accelerating from 0 to 400 km/h and back to 0 in just 27.83 seconds . Equipped with Koenigsegg's innovative 9-speed Light Speed Transmission (LST), the Jesko offers lightning-fast gear shifts and exceptional responsiveness. Its advanced aerodynamics, active rear-wheel steering, and lightweight carbon-fiber construction contribute to its unparalleled handling and stability at high speeds. With a limited production run of 125 units, all of which have been sold, the Koenigsegg Jesko stands as a testament to cutting-edge engineering and exclusivity in the hypercar segment.

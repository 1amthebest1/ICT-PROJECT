Rolls Royce

The Rolls-Royce Droptail epitomizes bespoke luxury, with only four unique units crafted, each reflecting its owner's vision. Powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbo V12 engine producing over 600 horsepower, it combines performance with opulence. The Droptail features a removable carbon-fiber roof with electrochromic glass, allowing seamless transition between coupe and roadster. Its interior showcases unparalleled craftsmanship, such as the La Rose Noire's 1,603-piece black sycamore parquetry, inspired by the Black Baccara rose. Priced upwards of $30 million, the Droptail stands as a testament to Rolls-Royce's commitment to personalized automotive artistry.

Ferrari F80

The Ferrari F80 is a revolutionary hybrid hypercar unveiled in 2025, blending cutting-edge performance with iconic Italian design. Inspired by Ferrari’s 499P Le Mans race car, the F80 features a hybrid twin-turbo V6 engine that delivers an astounding 1,184 horsepower.With advanced aerodynamics, a lightweight carbon-fiber body, and aggressive styling, it's engineered for extreme performance on both road and track. Limited to just 799 units—already sold out—the F80 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in under 2.5 seconds, firmly establishing its place as a new benchmark in the supercar world.
